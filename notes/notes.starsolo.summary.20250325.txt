== contents of STARsolo Summary.csv file.

URL: https://broadinstitute.github.io/warp/docs/Pipelines/Optimus_Pipeline/starsolo-metrics


Summary.csv:
  Sequencing Saturation: calculated as: 1-(yesUMIs/yessubWLmatch_UniqueFeature)

Features.stats:
  yesUMIs: Number of reads associated with a valid UMI
  yessubWLmatch_UniqueFeature: Number of reads matched to the WL and unique feature (a subset of yesWLmatch)
